Church Statistics

This app was made as an experiment in speed and styling. I got access to a spreadsheet with growth statistics for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints from 1990 to 2022 and wanted a quick way to visualize different metrics to satisfy my own curiosity. I also figured I could practice some shiny styling along the way. You'll notice that the styling is much more sleek and unified compared to previous apps thanks to the bslib library in R.



US Death Trends

This app is built on CDC data that I discovered while searching for alternative data for a regression class at BYU. It was my first attempt at building a shiny application and is primarily an interface for subsetting data and selecting metrics of interest. The primary benefit of the app is the ease of comparison across states or between individual states and the entire US for general death rates and cause-specific death rates.
