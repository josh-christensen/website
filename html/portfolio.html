Master's Project

  • My master's project deals with time series modeling in the Bayesian framework. The novel contributions are primarily concentrated in two research areas: Markov Chain Monte Carlo methods for sampling multivariate posterior distributions and dynamic harmonic regression models for time-variable cyclical time series. The pdf is linked below. After the presentation of my master's project we added a particle filter as a comparison case for the sampler. Moving forward I am interested in adding shrinkage priors to identify how many frequencies are necessary in the model and experimenting with more particle filtering and transformation-based samplers.
