Introduction

My name is Josh Christensen. I am approaching graduation from my MS in Statistics program at Brigham Young University and realized that I did not have a good way of sharing my technical portfolio. I have my github account, but I haven't used version control for all of my projects so it felt incompelete. I also wanted potential employers or others who are trying to evaluate my skills to have a place to be able to view my "finished products" without having to wade through source code in separate repositories. This website was created to fulfill that need.

The other two tabs include reports and shiny web applications that I have created either in school or for personal projects. Work that I have done for internships is not included due to the proprietary nature of the data and results, but many of the same skills are demonstrated through other projects. If you have any followup questions related to any of the projects you find here please feel free to reach out over LinkedIn.
